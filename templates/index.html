Fitme is set to transform the beauty landscape in Nigeria. Our on-demand salon and freelance stylist app connects you with top beauty professionals, offering a wide range of services from hair and makeup to nails and skincare. No more waiting in line at the salon—get pampered wherever you are, whenever you need it.

What is Fitme? Fitme is Nigeria's premier beauty service app, making it easier than ever to access professional beauty services. Whether you’re at home, at the office, or at a special event location, Fitme lets you book freelance stylists and salon experts on-demand. Plus, our integrated marketplace offers the best in beauty products, handpicked by professionals for Nigerian consumers.

  • Convenience: Say goodbye to traffic and long salon waits. Fitme brings the salon to you, allowing you to book services at your convenience, anywhere across Lagos, Abuja, Port Harcourt, and other major Nigerian cities.
  • Top Nigerian Talent: Our app features a network of carefully selected freelance beauty professionals, including hairstylists, makeup artists, nail technicians, and skincare experts. Each professional is vetted for quality and reliability.
  • Personalized Beauty Experience: Browse through profiles, view portfolios, read reviews, and choose the stylist that fits your unique style and beauty needs. Fitme ensures you have the perfect match for every occasion.
  • Marketplace: Discover a curated selection of beauty products tailored for the Nigerian market. From local favorites to international brands, shop for everything you need to maintain your beauty routine.

Join the Fitme Experience and elevate your beauty routine!
